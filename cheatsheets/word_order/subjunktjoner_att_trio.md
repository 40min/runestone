# 🇸🇪 Subjunktioner Cheatsheet — The "att" Trio

---

## 🔗 Genom att — *How / In what way*
> "By doing something..."

**Läraren motiverar eleverna genom att ge dem kaffe.**
*The teacher motivates students by giving them coffee.*

➡️ Use it when you explain **HOW** something happens.

---

## 🚫 Utan att — *Without doing something*
> "...without even trying"

**Läraren ställde frågor utan att någon svarade.**
*The teacher asked questions without anyone answering.*

➡️ Use it when someone does something and something else **does NOT** happen. Classic Monday energy.

---

## 🎯 För att — *Purpose / Goal*
> "In order to..."

**Läraren pratar om nyheter för att eleverna ska lära sig om livet.**
*The teacher talks about news so that students learn about life.*

➡️ Use it when you explain **WHY** or **what the goal is**.

---

## ⚡ Same Subject = Drop It!

When **both clauses share the same subject**, ditch the second subject and use **infinitive** instead.

| ❌ Redundant                                                | ✅ Clean                                               |
| ---------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Läraren motiverar eleverna **genom att hen ger** dem prov. | Läraren motiverar eleverna **genom att ge** dem prov. |

> Think of it as Swedish being lazy in the most elegant way possible. 😎

---

## 🧠 Quick Reminder

| Subjunktion   | Meaning       | Keyword    |
| ------------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| **genom att** | by doing      | HOW        |
| **utan att**  | without doing | NOT        |
| **för att**   | in order to   | WHY / GOAL |
