# 📊 Adverb: Komparation — Comparing with Adverbs

---

## 🌟 The Concept
> Just like adjectives, some adverbs have **three levels of intensity** — positive, comparative, superlative.
> Swedish said "why should adjectives have all the fun?" 🎉

---

## 🎯 The Classic Example: gärna

| Level | Word | Meaning | Vibe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Positiv | **gärna** | gladly / would like to | "Sure, I'd like that" 😊 |
| Komparativ | **hellre** | rather / more preferably | "I'd prefer this over that" 🤔 |
| Superlativ | **helst** | most preferably / ideally | "This is my dream, don't argue" 😤 |

**Rolf vill gärna bo i en villa.**
*Rolf would gladly live in a house.*

**Annika vill hellre bo i stan (än i en förort).**
*Annika would rather live in the city (than in a suburb).*

**Annika vill helst bo i en hyreslägenhet.**
*Annika would most of all love to live in a rental apartment.*

---

## 📍 Another one: nära

| Level | Word | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Positiv | **nära** | near / close |
| Komparativ | **närmare / närmre** | closer |
| Superlativ | **närmast / närmst** | closest / nearest |

**Butiken är nära.** → *The shop is close.*
**Den här butiken är närmare.** → *This shop is closer.*
**Vilken butik är närmast?** → *Which shop is nearest?*

---

## 🧠 Quick Cheat Card

| Positiv | Komparativ | Superlativ |
|---|---|---|
| gärna 😊 | hellre 🤔 | helst 😤 |
| nära 📍 | närmare 📍📍 | närmast 🎯 |

---

## ⚡ Pro tip — hellre always brings a friend

> **hellre** almost always comes with **än** (than) — it's a package deal 📦

**Jag vill hellre sova än studera.**
*I'd rather sleep than study.* *(relatable 😴)*
