# Swedish Komparation Cheat Sheet ☕

## The Holy Trinity of Comparison

| Basic | Comparative | Superlative |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| få (few) | färre (fewer) | **färst*** |
| många (many) | fler (more) | flest (most) |
| lite (little) | mindre (less) | minst (least) |
| mycket (much) | mer (more) | mest (most) |

**\*färst is apparently "incorrect but common" - the rebel of Swedish grammar!*
