# 📢 Presentering — Introducing New Information

---

## 🌟 The Formula
> Use this when something new enters the scene — like a narrator setting the stage 🎬

`det + verb + substantiv (obestämd form)`

- **det** = formal subject (just a placeholder, means nothing alone)
- **the noun** = the real subject (always **indefinite** — en/ett/no article)

> ⚠️ The noun is always **indefinite** — you're introducing it for the first time!
> *Det står **en** hörnsoffa...* ✅ — *Det står **hörnsoffan**...* ❌

---

## 1️⃣ Position verbs — står / ligger / sitter / hänger

> Something is *somewhere*, placed in a specific way 📍

**Det står en hörnsoffa vid öppen spis.**
*There's a corner sofa by the fireplace.*

**Det ligger många kuddar på soffan.**
*There are lots of cushions on the sofa.*

➡️ See the **Står/Ligger/Sitter/Hänger** cheatsheet for which verb to pick 😉

---

## 2️⃣ Existence verbs — finns / är

> Something simply *exists* or *is present* 🌍

**Det finns kakor i köket.**
*There are cookies in the kitchen.* *(important news 🍪)*

**Det är mycket folk på visningen.**
*There are a lot of people at the viewing.*

➡️ **finns** = exists somewhere | **är** = is present/there

---

## 3️⃣ Movement verbs — kommer / går / åker etc.

> Something is *moving into view* 🚂

**Stanna bilen! Det kommer ett tåg.**
*Stop the car! There's a train coming.*

**Det går en lös hund på gatan.**
*There's a stray dog walking on the street.*

---

## 🧠 Quick Cheat Table

| Category    | Verbs                        | Example                 |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| 📍 Position  | står, ligger, sitter, hänger | *Det står en lampa...*  |
| 🌍 Existence | finns, är                    | *Det finns en park...*  |
| 🏃 Movement  | kommer, går, åker, springer  | *Det kommer ett tåg...* |

---

## ⚡ Singular? Don't forget en/ett!

| En-word                 | Ett-word                |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Det kommer **en** buss. | Det kommer **ett** tåg. |
| Det finns **en** park.  | Det finns **ett** café. |

> Plural? No article needed: *Det ligger **många kuddar**...* 🛋️
