# ⚖️ Jämförelser — When Things Are Equal

---

## 🌟 The Formula: lika + (positiv) + som
> "Just as... as..." — for when you refuse to pick a side. 🤝

---

## 1️⃣ Basic equality — lika ... som

**Lägenheten är lika dyr som villan.**
*The apartment is just as expensive as the house.*

**Jag bor lika gärna i en lägenhet som i en villa.**
*I'm just as happy living in an apartment as in a house.*

➡️ Formula: `lika + adjective/adverb (positiv form) + som`

> 🆚 Compare with komparativ:
> **Villan är dyrare ÄN lägenheten.** *(not equal → än)*
> **Lägenheten är lika dyr SOM villan.** *(equal → som)*

| Equal                | Not equal              |
| -------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **lika** ... **som** | **-are/re** ... **än** |

---

## 2️⃣ Combining subjects — adjective goes plural

> When you merge the two things being compared into one sentence, the adjective gets **plural form**. They're a team now. 👯

**Lägenheten och villan är lika dyra.**
*The apartment and the house are equally expensive.*

➡️ Two subjects = adjective in **plural** (-a ending)

| One subject                           | Two subjects                           |
| ------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Lägenheten är lika dy**r** som villan | Lägenheten och villan är lika dy**ra** |

---

## 3️⃣ Comparing two phrases — adjective gets -t

> When both sides of the comparison are **full phrases** (not just nouns), the adjective takes the **-t form**. 🧩

**Att bo på landet är lika mysig*t* som att bo i stan.**
*Living in the countryside is just as cozy as living in the city.*

➡️ phrase + **lika** + adjective**-t** + **som** + phrase

---

## 🧠 Quick Cheat Table

| Situation             | Rule                    | Example          |
| --------------------- | ----------------------- | ---------------- |
| Comparing two nouns   | `lika + positiv + som`  | lika dyr **som** |
| Two subjects merged   | adjective in **plural** | lika dy**ra**    |
| Comparing two phrases | adjective with **-t**   | lika mysig**t**  |

---

## 🚨 lika...som vs. -are...än — Don't mix them up!

| 😌 Equal                | 😤 Not equal   |
| ---------------------- | ------------- |
| **lika** stor **som**  | **större än** |
| **lika** bra **som**   | **bättre än** |
| **lika** gärna **som** | **hellre än** |
