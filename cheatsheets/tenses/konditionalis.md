# Konditionalis 🎭 — "If only..." mode

Konditionalis describes a **hypothetical mood or meaning**, not a time by itself.
It is for things that are not presented as real: conditions, wishes, and imagined
outcomes. It often rides shotgun with an **om**-clause (*om* = *if*).

Two common flavors depend on *when* the unreal or hypothetical thing lives.

---

## Konditionalis 1 — "still possible" 🌤️

**Use when:** the condition concerns the present or future. It is hypothetical,
but it could still happen. Hope is not dead yet.

**Formula:** `skulle + infinitive` (main clause) + `om + past tense`
(if-clause)

| Swedish | English | Vibe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jag **skulle bli** glad om jag **fick** 100 000 prenumeranter | I would be happy if I got 100k subscribers | still grindin' 📈 |
| Om jag **hade** mer tid **skulle** jag **göra** fler videor | If I had more time, I'd make more videos | time, the eternal excuse ⏰ |
| Om jag **vore** sjuk **skulle** jag **stanna** hemma | If I were sick, I'd stay home | universal law of duvet physics 🛌 |

📝 The **om**-clause can come first or second. If it comes first, the main
clause uses normal Swedish inversion: `Om jag hade tid, skulle jag...`

💡 For an open, likely condition rather than a hypothetical one, Swedish usually
uses the present tense: `Om jag får 100 000 prenumeranter blir jag glad.`

---

## Konditionalis 2 — "dead and buried" ⚰️

**Use when:** the condition concerns the past, and it did **not** happen. No
amount of wishing fixes this one.

**Formula:** `skulle ha + supine` (main clause) + `om + hade + supine`
(if-clause)

| Swedish | English | Vibe |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jag **skulle ha köpt** en ny bil om jag **hade vunnit** en miljon | I would've bought a new car if I'd won a million | lottery dreams 🎰 |
| Jag **skulle ha tagit** en promenad igår om vädret **hade varit** fint | I would've gone for a walk yesterday if the weather had been nice | blaming the sky, a Swedish national pastime ☔ |
| Jag **skulle ha gjort** läxan om jag **hade haft** tid | I would've done the homework if I'd had time | relatable in any language 📚 |

---

## Konditionalis 2: three ways to express the same regret 🔁

All three can express the same unreal past outcome, but the context and register
differ:

| Style | Example | When to use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **skulle ha + supine** | Jag skulle ha köpt en bil om jag hade haft råd | neutral, clear, and safe |
| **hade + supine** instead of *skulle ha* | Jag hade köpt en bil om jag hade haft råd | standard alternative when the condition makes the meaning clear |
| **skulle + supine** (drop *ha*) | Jag skulle köpt en bil om jag hade haft råd | common in speech and informal writing; avoid in a formal essay |

---

## The cheat-cheat 🧠

| | Time | Reality check | Structure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Kond. 1** | now / future | hypothetical, but still possible | `skulle + infinitive` |
| **Kond. 2** | past | impossible now: over and done | `skulle ha + supine` |

Rule of thumb: if it *could still* happen → Kond. 1. If the universe already
closed that door → Kond. 2, light a candle 🕯️.
