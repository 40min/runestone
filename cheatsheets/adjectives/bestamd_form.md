# 🎨 Adjektiv: Bestämd Form — When Adjectives Get Formal

---

## 📊 All Forms at a Glance

|                                         | EN-ORD    | ETT-ORD   | PLURAL    |
| --------------------------------------- | --------- | --------- | --------- |
| **OBESTÄMD** (before noun / after verb) | stor      | stor**t** | stor**a** |
| **BESTÄMD** (before noun)               | stor**a** | stor**a** | stor**a** |

> 💡 Bestämd form = always **-a**. Just one form to remember. Swedish throws you a bone. 🦴

---

## 🌟 When to Use Bestämd Form

### 1️⃣ After definite articles & demonstrative pronouns
> den, det, de, den där, det där, de där, denna, detta, dessa...

**den ny**a** kaffeapparaten** — *the new coffee maker*
**den där gaml**a** soffan** — *that old sofa*

➡️ Definite/demonstrative before noun = adjective gets **-a** 🔒

---

### 2️⃣ After possessives & names (noun stays INDEFINITE!)
> min, din, sin, vår, er, deras + names

**vår röd**a** soffa** — *our red sofa* (not: *soffan*!)
**Jennys ny**a** kille** — *Jenny's new boyfriend*

➡️ Possessive/name = adjective **-a**, BUT the noun stays **indefinite** 👀

> ⚠️ This trips everyone up:
> | ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
> |---|---|
> | vår röda soff**an** | vår röda soff**a** |

---

### 3️⃣ After a verb — always INDEFINITE 🚫
> är, ser ut, blir, verkar...

**Den gamla apparaten var ganska dålig.**
*The old appliance was pretty bad.*

➡️ **den gamla** ✅ (before noun) BUT **var dålig** ✅ not *dåliga* — after verb = obestämd!

---

### 4️⃣ No article needed — special cases

**4a. Direct address** — talking or writing TO someone:

**Snälla farmor**, kan jag ta en kaka till?
*Dear grandma, can I have another cookie?*

**Kära Pelle!** Här i Paris har vi det bra.
*Dear Pelle! We're doing well here in Paris.*

**4b. Proper names** — just how they are:

**Gamla stan** • **Nordiska museet** • **Europeiska unionen**

---

## 🔬 SPECIAL CASE: liten — The Rebel 😤

|              | EN-ORD    | ETT-ORD   | PLURAL  |
| ------------ | --------- | --------- | ------- |
| **OBESTÄMD** | lite**n** | lite**t** | **små** |
| **BESTÄMD**  | li**lla** | li**lla** | **små** |

**en liten hund** → **den lilla hunden** 🐕
**ett litet hus** → **det lilla huset** 🏠
**små barn** → **de små barnen** 👶

> *liten* said "I refuse to follow rules" and invented *lilla* and *små*. Respect. 😤

---

## 🧠 Quick Decision Tree

```
Adjective before noun?
├── After den/det/de/den där...?  → BESTÄMD (-a)
├── After min/din/vår/Jenny's...? → BESTÄMD (-a), noun INDEFINITE
└── After a verb (är, ser ut...)?  → OBESTÄMD (stor/stort/stora)

No article, special cases?
├── Addressing someone directly?  → BESTÄMD (-a)
└── Part of a proper name?        → BESTÄMD (-a)
```
